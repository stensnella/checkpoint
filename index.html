Checkpoint

The safer way to tramp.

Checkpoint! The perfect app for anyone wanting to be safe out on the track. They say you should always let someone know when you are going out into the bush, but now Checkpoint does the work for you.


Checkpoint allows you to input your track information such as expected departure and arrival time, which track you are walking on, tramping experience and emergency contacts. If you don't check in after your estimated arrival time, a customisable alert message will be sent out to your emergency contact.


If you are running late, don't worry, just let Checkpoint know your new estimated arrival time so no one has to get worried.


Create a profile to save emergency contacts, previous tracks and more!

Check in


Checkpoint sends an alert message to your emergency contact if you don't check in on time.

If you check in on time, Checkpoint registers that you're safe and doesn't notify an emergency contact.
